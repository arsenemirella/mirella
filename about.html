WHO IS THE WEEKND?

The Weeknd, born Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. His unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music has garnered critical acclaim and a massive global fanbase. Beyond the mainstream hits, The Weeknd's artistry delves into introspective themes, creating a distinctive musical experience for listeners.
