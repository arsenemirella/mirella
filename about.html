WHO IS THE WEEKND?

The Weeknd is a critically acclaimed artist known for his unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music. With hits like "Blinding Lights" and "Starboy," he has captivated audiences worldwide. His music goes beyond conventional genres, offering a rich tapestry of sounds that resonate with diverse audiences.
